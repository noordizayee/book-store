Stephen King

Stephen King is one of the most prolific and well-known
authors of contemporary horror, supernatural fiction, suspense, and fantasy.
Born on September 21, 1947, in Portland, Maine,
King has published over 60 novels, numerous short stories, and even non-fiction works.
His books have sold more than 350 million copies worldwide,
many of which have been adapted into films, television series, and miniseries
