Nora Robert

Nora Roberts is a prolific American author
known for her romance novels, romantic suspense, and fantasy fiction.
Born on October 10, 1950, in Silver Spring, Maryland, Roberts has written over 200 novels,
many of which have appeared on the New York Times bestseller list
